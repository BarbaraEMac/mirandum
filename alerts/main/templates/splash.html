Livestream Alerts

Livestream Alerts is a tool for YouTube Gaming broadcasters designed to let you include alerts of various events directly into your stream. It takes less than 5 minutes to set up, so you can be rewarding viewers for donations and subscriptions right away!

  • Support for YouTube subscriber notifications
  • Support for YouTube sponsor notifications (for users in the Sponsorship beta)
  • Support for all commonly used livestreaming donation platforms:
    • YouTube Fan Funding
    • TwitchAlerts
    • Imraising
    • StreamTip
  • Chrome app support for downloading lists of recent activity and top donations
  • Recent donations across all platforms: no more "biggest D" and "Biggest Chat D" needed!
  • Ability to easily upload your own custom sounds and images.
  • Support for advanced animations like sliding/bouncing etc. to add a kick to your alerts.
  • Coming Soon: Cross-platform donation goal support
