When selecting an account to connect for subscriber notifications, it should be a Gmail account. For users who use a Google+ Page (e.g. if you don't use your real name for your YouTube Account), this usually means it will be different from your YouTube account.

You must enable subscription notifications to be delivered over email. Visit your Notification Settings and ensure "Send me emails about my YouTube activity (except emails I've unsubscribed from)" is checked; and you do not have any Unsubscribed notfications.

When you reach 1000 subscribers, you will need to visit this page again, since new subscriber emails are automatically disabled when you reach 1000 subs.
